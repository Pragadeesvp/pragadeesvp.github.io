About

Who we are

Sri Shanmuga Packaging is one of the leading provider of packaging solutions in diverse sectors that range from Food & Beverages, Industrial Engineering, Automobile Industries, Electronic Appliances, Textile & Garments and Furniture. Since its inception in 2017, Sri Shanmuga Packaging has grown from a product-oriented company into a market-oriented company. We deliver value-added products for which our customers just love to work with us. We have rich experience in servicing top-of-the-line specialized products to the printing & packaging industry. Sri Shanmuga Packaging operates from Pollachi, TamilNadu.
